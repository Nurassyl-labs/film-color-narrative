The Color Fingerprints of Movie Genres

While a single movie's barcode reveals its visual identity, wider patterns emerge when we aggregate colors across dozens of films of the same genre. We selected the top 25 movies by IMDb vote count for each genre (randomly assigning multi-genre films to a single category) and calculated their average colors in the HSL color space. Each genre exhibits distinct color preferences—for instance, Animation tends to have bright, pastel-like palettes, while Horror films lean heavily toward dark, earthy browns.
