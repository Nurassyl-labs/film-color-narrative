A. Intro

A Poetry of Color in Time Slices

Cinema is the art of light and shadow. If we aggregate the dominant colors of a film across small intervals and arrange them chronologically into a continuous band, we get a film color barcode. It strips away concrete images and sound, leaving only the essence of color. We will begin our exploration of color narratives in film from this barcode.

The color barcode of the classic film "The Dark Knight". Each color block represents the dominant hue of the frame over a period of time, revealing the film's characteristically dark and gritty color palette.
