Color Breathing with the Narrative

In the previous section (B), we explored the link between color and film genres. Now we go a step further to uncover the intrinsic connection between color and story progression. We selected movies from the Animation, Action, and Horror genres and segmented their plots based on subtitle timeline analysis. For each segment, we aggregated visual color, emotional sentiment, and dialogue density. A close harmony between narrative progression and visual tone emerges. For instance, in "The Matrix", the first act is dominated by a dark, green-tinted atmosphere of tension; the second act brightens as the protagonist awakens; and the final act shifts to a stark, desaturated color palette that represents the bleakness of the real world. Color functions as an active participant in narrative rhythm.
